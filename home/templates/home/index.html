About

The Dine Restaurant is a beautifully classic, yet stylish and relaxed all-day dining restaurant in the heart of The Stockholm,Sweden. we want our guests to feel well taken care of and to be served good food. Our philosophy is simplicity and quality. We look forward to welcoming you.

Restaurateur karl Johan creates together with chef Magnus Daniellson a menu where the flavors of the season are clearly displayed. All ingredients are carefully selected and we work as closely as possible with nearby producers.

In the dining room, you are looked after by fellow restaurateur and restaurant manager Linus Ljunquist. Together with his dining room team, he has made wine suggestions for each dish and is responsible for a solid wine list with over 800 different wines. Karl Johan and Linus Ljungquist with staff
